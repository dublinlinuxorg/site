The Dublin Linux Community

Meetup Schedule

We’re a community of like-minded individuals in Dublin with a passion for Linux and all things free (as in free speech) and open source software.

Whether you’re a seasoned Linux user or someone who would simply like to learn more please feel free to come along to one of our regular meetups or follow us through one of our social accounts below.
If chatting is more your thing you can find us on Telegram. We are a pretty active group!

Want to help out?

We're all volunteers in the community so any skills you feel you could contribute would always be welcome, even if you aren't sure how you could help out but are still willing. Non technical skills such as design, event organisation, PR as well as technical skills will all be considered. Contact us via our socials above if you're interested!
